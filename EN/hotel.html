Bang Krang Homestay

If you're visiting Phang Nga and looking for a cozy place to stay with a beautiful mangrove forest atmosphere, this is the perfect spot for you. Not only does this homestay offer great accommodations, but you can also ask the staff to take you on a special dining experience at a hidden restaurant deep within the mangrove forest. You'll enjoy a scenic boat ride to a floating restaurant, where you can savor delicious specialties like soft-shell crab at an affordable price, along with fresh seafood sourced daily from the sea. If you're looking for a truly unique and relaxing experience, come unwind here and immerse yourself in nature.
